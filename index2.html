School is a venue to acquire knowledge. It is also an essential starting point to cultivate environmental protection concepts for the next generation. School plays an indispensable role in promoting environmental education. Environmental education, including knowledge, skills, attitudes, or values taught by teachers in the classroom, profoundly affects the next generation. From kindergarten to university, from indoors to outdoors, environmental education can be accessed and promoted anywhere. The Environmental Protection Administration (EPA) and the Ministry of Education have been working together for years to actively promote environmental education in schools. Various competitions and activities are organized to enhance students’ environmental knowledge, kindle their learning interest, and encourage them to further explore the environment. These experiences are crucial for students to be aware of the importance of environmental protection and to implement related measures in daily life.
